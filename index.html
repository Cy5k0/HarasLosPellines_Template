About Us

We are a Chilean family from the south that combines traditional agriculture with the breeding of exceptional Arabian horses.

Location

Polloico, Osorno, Chile

Surroundings

Located on the northern edge of Chilean Patagonia, our farm is situated in a privileged environment. The region is characterized by its green pastures, where the quantity and quality of grass are exceptional, remaining lush for much of the year. Winters are wet and cold, providing an ideal climate for various equestrian activities, while summers are sunny, perfect for enjoying the natural beauty of the area.

Services

Horse Sales

If you are interested in any of our specimens, please feel free to contact us for more details. Our Arabian horses come with all the documentation that certifies their lineage.

Genetic Material for Sale

In our breeding facility, we offer the highest quality equine genetic material for those looking to improve or diversify their breeding program. We have embryos and semen from exceptional lineage Arabian horses, carefully selected to ensure the best genetic traits.

Equine Lodging

In our breeding facility, we offer first-class equine lodging services, ideal for those seeking exceptional care for their horses. Located on the northern edge of Patagonia, in Osorno, we have extensive pastures and abundant fresh grass, providing a natural and healthy environment for the horses.

Our Horses

In our breeding facility, we have an exceptionally well-equipped stable that houses a select group of Arabian horses. Our stable consists of 16 Arabian horses, all of them from distinguished lineage and cared for with the utmost dedication.
